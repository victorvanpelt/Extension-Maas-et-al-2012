  • This study consists of two parts: an interactive part and a non-interactive part.
  • We start with the interactive part and will clearly mention when we start with the non-interactive part. The decisions you take during the interactive part do not have an influence on the non-interactive part and vice versa.
  • The interactive part consists of 8 rounds. Every round has the same sequence of events.
  • You will be matched with two other participants of this session. Two participants will act as ‘business unit manager’ (BU manager from here onwards). One participant will act as ‘Supervisor’. The computer randomly determines whether you will act as BU manager or Supervisor.
  • At the start of each round, you will be rematched with two randomly chosen participants of this session.
  • Some, but not all, participants will change roles (from BU manager to Supervisor or from Supervisor to BU manager) after round 4. The decisions you make during the first 4 rounds do not affect the chance that your role will change after round 4.
  • At the end of the experiment, the computer will randomly select one of the 8 rounds to determine your compensation for the interactive part.
