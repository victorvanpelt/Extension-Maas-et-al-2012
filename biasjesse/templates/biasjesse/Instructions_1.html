  • This study consists of two separate parts, an interactive part and a non-interactive part, which do not influence each other.
  • We will start with the interactive part which consists of 8 rounds. Every round has the same sequence of events.
  • At the start of every round, the computer will match you to two randomly chosen participants in this session.
  • Two of you will act as ‘business unit manager’ (hereafter BU manager), and one of you will act as ‘Supervisor’. The computer also randomly determines whether you will act as BU manager or Supervisor.
  • Some, but not all, participants will change roles (from BU manager to Supervisor or from Supervisor to BU manager) after round 4. The decisions you make during the first 4 rounds do not affect the chance that your role will change after round 4.
  • At the end of the experiment, the computer will randomly select one of the 8 rounds to determine your compensation in Lira for the interactive part.
