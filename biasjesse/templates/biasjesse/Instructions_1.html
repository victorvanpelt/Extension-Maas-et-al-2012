  • The two BU managers each receive 10 Lira, which is the currency we use in this experiment.
  • The BU managers will need to decide how much of it to invest in a collaborative project with the other BU manager.
  • The amount of Lira that is not invested, the BU managers keep for themselves.
  • The invested amount is combined with the amount invested by the other BU manager, which jointly is multiplied by 1.5 to determine the joint bonus pool for the BU managers.
  • The supervisor decides how to divide the total bonus pool amongst the two BU managers.
  • The total reward of a BU manager equals the not invested amount plus the bonus allocated by the supervisor.
