  • At the start of each round, each BU manager receives 10 Lira.
  • Each BU manager decides how much of the 10 Lira to invest in a collaborative project with the other BU manager. This is the individual investment of a BU manager.
  • Each BU managers keeps the amount of Lira that is not invested in the collaborative project for him/herself.
  • The individual investments of both business unit managers are added up and is the total investment. The total investment is multiplied by 1.5. This number is the total bonus pool for the BU managers. The total bonus pool is thus based on the individual investment of each BU manager.
  • The Supervisor decides how to divide the total bonus pool amongst the two BU managers. The Supervisor can choose any possible division of the total bonus pool.
