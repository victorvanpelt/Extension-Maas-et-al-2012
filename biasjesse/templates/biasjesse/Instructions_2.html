  • At the start of each round, each of the two BU managers receives 10 Lira.

  • The sum of the BU managers' individual investments (hereafter the total investment) is multiplied by 1.5 to determine the total bonus pool for the BU managers.
  • The Supervisor decides how to divide the total bonus pool among the two BU managers. The Supervisor can choose any possible division in percentages of the total bonus pool.
