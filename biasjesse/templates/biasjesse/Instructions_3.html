  • Specifically, the computer compares the Supervisor’s willingness to pay to a random number between 0 Lira and 5 Lira.

    • If the Supervisor’s willingness to pay ≥ the random number, he or she receives the information and his or her reward is: 15 Lira – random number.
  • If the Supervisor’s willingness to pay is 0 Lira, he or she does not receive the information and his or her reward is: 15 Lira.
  • If the Supervisor’s willingness to pay is 5 Lira, he or she receives the information and his or her reward is: 15 Lira – 5 Lira = 10 Lira.
  • Note that the Supervisor's reward does not depend on the individual investments nor on how he or she allocates the bonus pool.
