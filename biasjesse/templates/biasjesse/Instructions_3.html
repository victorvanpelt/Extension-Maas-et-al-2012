  • The study consists of 8 rounds, and at the start of each new round, new groups of three participants (one supervisor and two BU managers) will be formed using a random process.
  • Some, but not all, participants will change roles (from BU managers to supervisor or the other way around) after round 4.
  • The choices you make during the study do NOT affect the chance that your role will change after round 4.
  • If the supervisor gets access to the information, then (s)he can, but is not obliged to, use this information in dividing the bonus pool over the BU managers.
  • After round 8, you will be asked to fill in a short questionnaire.
  • After you filled in the questionnaire, the computer will randomly select one of the 8 rounds to determine the compensation in Euro for all participants.
  • Specifically, the total reward in a round is specified in Lira. However, after randomly selecting the total reward in a particular round, it is converted to Euro using a 1 Lira to 0.50 Euro ratio.
